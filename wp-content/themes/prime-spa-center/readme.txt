== Description ==

Prime Spa Center is an ultimate solution for your online spa business needs! With its impressive range of features, including Blog Options, Footer Options, Share Options, Custom CSS, SEO Options, and Woocommerce readiness, this theme has everything you need to create a top-notch website.With the Prime Spa Center theme, you can easily create a website that highlights the benefits of your spa services. Our theme focuses on benefits rather than features, ensuring that your website visitors are enticed by the luxurious experiences you offer.The Blog Options feature allows you to create engaging and informative blog posts that showcase your spa's expertise in beauty and wellness. Additionally, the Footer Options feature lets you customize your website's footer to match your branding and provide additional information to your website visitors.With the Share Options feature, your website visitors can easily share your content on their social media platforms, which helps to increase your spa's online visibility. The Custom CSS feature allows you to further customize your website's appearance and create a unique online presence for your spa. Our theme also offers robust SEO options, ensuring that your website ranks high on search engines like Google. This means more traffic to your website, and ultimately, more business for your spa.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
